# AD5761

[Datasheet](https://www.analog.com/media/en/technical-documentation/data-sheets/ad5761r_5721r.pdf)

## SPI

- Max clock 50 MHz.
- Output ranges used:
  - ±3
  - ±5
  - ±10

The output voltage range is adjustable digitally. It's good for dealing with the
precision required to scan atoms and the variation of the thermal drift. But the
interplay is more complicated due the coarse approach. For now let's just assume
a range of ±10 and move on. It is more important to allow one range than to
settle ground for all three.
