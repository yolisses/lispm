# Vibration isolation

Some other projects use spring suspension systems with aluminium and magnets for damping. While effective, that kind of setup takes up a lot of space and can be costly due to custom-made parts.  
I decided to follow the design used in the first STM and validated in Vitor Barbosa's STM: a heavy plate resting on top of an inner tube. The air-filled cavity blocks high-frequency vibrations, since they dissipate into the gas rather than reaching the scan head.  
On top of that, I plan to add a magnetic levitation system like [this one](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ML4FHNGzAn0), integrated into the shielding box to keep things compact.
